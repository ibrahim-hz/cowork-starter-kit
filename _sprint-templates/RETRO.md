# Sprint <N> — Retrospective

Written at sprint end. Terse is fine — nobody reads retros that are more than one screen.

- **Sprint:** <N> — <theme>
- **Dates:** YYYY-MM-DD → YYYY-MM-DD
- **Prompts planned / executed:** X / Y

## What shipped

Bullet list of the actual user-visible outcomes. Not "refactored the X component" — "users can now upload a document from the mobile app."

## What worked

What Cowork or Claude Code did well that you want to keep doing. Examples: plan sizing was right, a particular prompt structure produced clean code, a specific pattern avoided rework.

## What didn't work

What you want to change next sprint. Be concrete — "Prompt 4 was too big and ran out of context" beats "prompts were too big."

## Surprises

Bugs you didn't anticipate, scope you underestimated, decisions that turned out wrong. These are the most valuable retro items — they feed future ADRs and build-plan sizing.

## Carryover

One-line pointer to `CARRYOVER.md` if anything shifted to the next sprint. If no carryover, say so explicitly ("nothing deferred").
